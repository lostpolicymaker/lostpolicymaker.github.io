### Conferences

#### ![pins](/img/mapicons/blackhatpin.png) Black Hat USA 2026
`August 1-4, 2026 (Trainings)`

`August 5-6, 2026 (Briefings)`

`where:` ([Mandalay Bay Convention Center](https://maps.google.com/?q=Mandalay+Bay+Convention+Center+Las+Vegas))

`web:` https://blackhat.com/us-26/

The [Black Hat](https://blackhat.com/us-26/) conference features talks about security issues and approaches that impact enterprises and corporations. [Learn more about Black Hat...](blackhat)

#### ![pins](/img/mapicons/bsidespin.png) BSides Las Vegas 2026
`August 3-5, 2026`

`where:` ([Tuscany Suites and Casino](https://maps.google.com/?q=Tuscany+Suites+and+Casino+Las+Vegas))

`web:` https://bsideslv.org

[BSides Las Vegas](https://bsideslv.org) is the original in a series of hundreds of global annual events, founded in 2009 as an 'un-conference' style event and a more laid-back alternative to DEF CON and Black Hat. [Learn more about BSides Las Vegas...](bsideslv)

#### ![pins](/img/mapicons/defconpin.png) DEF CON 34
`August 6-9, 2026`

`where:` ([Las Vegas Convention Center, West Hall](https://maps.google.com/?q=Las+Vegas+Convention+Center+West+Hall))

`web:` https://defcon.org

[DEF CON](https://defcon.org) is the premiere global hacker conference. Even after more than 30 years, DEF CON remains an open space for intellectual exploration, open to all those who want to participate in good faith. [Learn more about DEF CON...](defcon)

#### ![pins](/img/mapicons/dianainipin.png) The Diana Initiative
`During DEF CON week`

`web:` https://www.dianainitiative.org/

[The Diana Initiative](https://www.dianainitiative.org/) is a diversity-driven non-profit, launched in 2016, committed to helping all underrepresented people in Information Security. They now host TDI @ DEF CON activities, meetups, and a Quiet Room during the conference. [Check their events page for this year's schedule...](https://www.dianainitiative.org/events)

### Before You Go
* Update all your technology
* Buy and test a new, clean phone, with new accounts (optional)
* Register for conference secure wifi
* Prepare to stand and walk a lot (bring comfy shoes)
* Rest up for a few days before
* Earmark some talks, villages, and events
* Schedule time to eat and sleep
* Download the Hacker Tracker app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shortstack.hackertracker&hl=en_US), [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hackertracker/id1021141595))
* Pack: ear plugs, sunscreen, shorts, sunglasses
* Check out some Hacker Films (see [Hacker Culture 101](hackerculture101))
* Join and participate in the [DEF CON Forums](https://forum.defcon.org)
